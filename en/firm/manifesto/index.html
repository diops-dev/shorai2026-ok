Manifesto

We believe AI is not a product. It is a discipline.

We believe rigour is more valuable than speed, and that scoped slowness beats systematic haste.

We believe the best AI projects often begin with a recommendation to do nothing: not yet, not like this.

We believe compliance is not a brake but a framework. Without a framework, there is no durable production.

We believe transparency on costs, timelines, risks and blind spots is non-negotiable. Including when it costs us an engagement.

We believe SMEs and mid-market companies deserve the same scoping methods as large groups, without the useless overhead.

We believe in our clients' autonomy. What we deliver must be able to live without us.
