Expertise 02

Generative AI transformation

From pilot to production. No illusions, no turning back.

Why

GenAI pilots are easy. Production is less so.

Between the two, 80% of projects are lost on data quality, change management, recurring costs and the absence of business ownership.

Our typical workstreams

RAG architecture

Indexing, retrieval, guardrails, observability. Not a POC, a robust pipeline.

Business copilots

Integration with the tools in place (CRM, ERP, email). Adoption measured, not assumed.

Process automation

With the napkin test upstream, ROI calculations downstream.

Continuous evaluation

Quality metrics, drift, hallucinations, inference costs.

Our conviction

Three conditions, non-negotiable.

A use case that solves a real problem, reliable data to feed it, a business team that owns it. When one of these three is missing, we say so before we start.
