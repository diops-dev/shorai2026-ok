An honest assessment. A realistic roadmap. A reasoned go/no-go.

Why

Most AI projects fail before the first line of code.

Blurred scope, missing data, no sponsor, fantasised ROI. Our role is to make it objective before a single euro is spent.

What you get

Process mapping

Which functions, which data, which bottlenecks.

Risk mapping

Prioritised roadmap
